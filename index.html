About the Program

Our program blends creativity and technology. Students learn design thinking, modern tools, and professional practices while building a portfolio that opens doors to college and careers across graphic design, web, and multimedia.

  • Hands-on learning with Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Real client projects and community engagement
  • College credit and certification opportunities

Who It's For

Students
